---

Tavus Speech lets you generate lifelike audio from text using a Replica’s voice. Just provide your script and a `callback_url`. Once the speech is ready, Tavus sends a callback with a download link to the audio file that can be used for lipsync or video generation.

## Key Features
<CardGroup cols={2}>

  <Card title="Lifelike Speech Generation" icon="volume">
    Produces expressive, human-like speech tailored to your selected Replica's voice characteristics.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Seamless Video Compatibility" icon="film">

  </Card>

</CardGroup>
