Tavus enables you to create AI-driven conversations through the Conversational Video Interface (CVI) pipeline, combining custom personas and replicas to deliver highly engaging, human-like interactions.

## Conversational Video Interface (CVI)

<Frame caption="CVI (Conversational Video Interface) Pipeline">
![](/images/tavus-101.png)
</Frame>

[Conversational Video Interface (CVI)](/sections/conversational-video-interface/overview-cvi) acts as an **end-to-end pipeline** for real-time multimodal video conversations, enabling a replica to see, hear, and respond like a human. It uses modular layers, each configurable through Persona settings to suit your specific use case.

<Card title="Conversations" href="/sections/conversational-video-interface/conversation/overview" icon="video">
