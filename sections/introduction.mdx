Tavus enables you to create AI-driven conversations through the Conversational Video Interface (CVI) pipeline, combining custom personas and replicas to deliver highly engaging, human-like interactions.

<CardGroup>
<Card title="CVI (Conversational Video Interface)" href="/sections/conversational-video-interface/overview-cvi" icon="code">
**End-to-end pipeline** for real-time multimodal video conversations, enabling a replica to see, hear, and respond like a human.
</Card>
<Card title="Conversations" href="/sections/conversational-video-interface/conversation/overview" icon="comments">
Real-time, **two-way video interaction** between a user and a Tavus Replica — powered by AI to feel natural, personalized, and human-like.
</Card>
<Card title="Personas" href="/sections/conversational-video-interface/persona/overview" icon="user-pen">
**The character of an AI agent**, shaping its behavior, communication style, and interaction patterns, enabling **customization of CVI layers** such as perception, speech recognition, LLM, and text-to-speech.
</Card>
<Card title="Replicas" href="/sections/replica/overview" icon="users-rectangle">
**Hyper-realistic digital humans** powered by AI models, capable of speaking, moving, and conversing naturally.
</Card>
</CardGroup>
