Tavus lets you generate high-quality AI videos using **Replica**. Whether you're producing personalized outreach, marketing content, or AI-driven demos, the **Phoenix model** turns your scripts into photorealistic talking-head videos in just minutes.


## Key Features
- **Photorealistic Output**: Generates lifelike talking-head videos from Well-written scripts improve tone, pacing, and realism.
- **Background Customizations**: Replace or modify the video background to suit your needs.
- **Fast Processing**: Videos are ready within minutes, depending on script length.
- **Dual Inputs**: Supports both text and audio scripts.
- **Script-Driven Control**: Script phrasing influences tone, pacing, and expressions.
- **Sharable & Downloadable**: Get a hosted video URL or download the video directly.

<Note>
**Note**:
- Token usage is based on video duration.  
- Output can vary slightly even with the same script and Replica.  
</Note>

## Getting Started
You can create a personalized video content using the [Tavus Platform](https://platform.tavus.io/) or by following the steps in the [Video Generation API Quickstart Guide](/sections/video/quickstart).
